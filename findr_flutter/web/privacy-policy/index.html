Privacy Policy for Wayvio

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Information Collection & Use

Wayvio processes data to provide local search results.

Location Data: We request access to your precise location (GPS) to find nearby stores. This is processed locally and sent securely to mapping APIs (Google Maps, Overpass API). We do not store your location history on our servers.

Search Queries: Searches are processed to provide results and AI recommendations.

Local Storage: Your search history, favorites, and notes are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

We utilize the following services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services & Google Maps API
  • SerpApi & Overpass API

Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit. We implement safety filters to block illegal or dangerous search terms.
